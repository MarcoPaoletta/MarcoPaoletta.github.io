ABOUT ME

Videogame developer

  • I create my own video games in which I spend most of my time without neglecting my high school studies.

MY JOURNEY AS A PROGRAMMER

Python

I started using Python in January 2021 when I was 13 years old because I was curious about how to create a videogame. I did few projects since I felt constantly limited and that I could give more of myself

GDScript

Feeling limited by Python, I started using Godot Engine whose GDScript language has Python-like syntax. I worked with Godot for about 1 year and learned how to use it efficiently

HTML - CSS - JS

As I was having more and more projects, I learned the basics to create the Front-end part of web pages. This knowledge was applied, for example, in this same portfolio website

C#

After knowing the basics of web development, I began to investigate other game engines. So, I discovered Unity and its C# language: highly demanded and used for famous video games nowadays

Google Play

A couple of months later, my skills to use Unity increased as well as my desire to publish video games in an official store. Finally, on October 11, 2022, I released Swipe Never Stop on the Google Play Store
